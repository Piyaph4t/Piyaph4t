I’m a curious engineering student passionate about understanding how systems truly work — from the lowest levels of computing to complex software architectures.

I don’t just want to know *that* something works.
I want to understand *why* it works.

That mindset led me into computer engineering, reverse engineering, cybersecurity, and low-level programming. I enjoy breaking systems apart, studying their internals, and rebuilding my understanding from first principles — no black boxes.

I started programming at 12 years old, and over the past 3 years, the journey has shaped the way I think, learn, and engineer solutions. Every project, bug, and failure has pushed me closer to becoming the kind of engineer I aspire to be.

My long-term goal is to contribute to world-class engineering teams working on advanced technology, security, AI, and large-scale systems.
